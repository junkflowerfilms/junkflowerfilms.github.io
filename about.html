our co - founders s --

Keya Saxena l & l Rose Knopper

began their friendship & creative partnership out of a small railroad apartment where they shared a room in their sophomore year at NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts. Both named after flowers, they began their filmmaking journey from the ground up, planting their seed in today’s creative spheres as a long-standing director & cinematographer duo.

With numerous accolades including an Oscar-Qualifying acceptance as well as 15+ film festivals, awards such as the Wasserman Center, Akhtar-Bhutta Student Producer Grants & the DGA Jury Prize, the Tony Hawkins Award and several more - their collaborations have grown to cultivate a shared voice of their own.
