Keya Saxena (she/her) & Rose Knopper (she/they) began their friendship & creative partnership out of a small railroad apartment where they shared a room in their sophmore year at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts.

With numerous accolades including an Oscar-Qualifying acceptance as well as 15+ film festivals, awards such as the Wasserman Center, Akhtar-Bhutta Student Producer Grants & the DGA Jury Prize, the Tony Hawkins Award and several more - their collaborations have grown to cultiivate a shared voice of their own.

Both named after flowers, they began their filmmaking journey from the ground up, planting their seed in today's creative spheres as a long-standing director & cinematographer duo.
